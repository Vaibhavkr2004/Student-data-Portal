School of Computer Engineering | KIIT

KIIT is a prestigious educational institution offering world-class academic programs. With a campus spread over 36 square kilometers, KIIT is home to over 40,000 students, including 2,000 international students from 65+ countries.

Campus Infrastructure

KIIT boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, including a super-specialty hospital (KIMS), a multi-story central library, modern auditoriums, 18 sports complexes, and international-standard stadiums.

Research & Innovation

KIIT is dedicated to fostering research and innovation. It houses numerous research centers focusing on AI, Robotics, Biotechnology, and more. The university encourages students and faculty to collaborate on groundbreaking projects.

Student Life

KIIT provides a vibrant student life with cultural events, technical fests, sports tournaments, and numerous clubs. Students from diverse backgrounds come together to create an engaging and dynamic community.
